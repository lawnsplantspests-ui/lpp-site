Frequently Asked Questions

Are you locally owned?
Yes. Lawns Plants & Pests LLC is locally owned and family-run. The same person who answers the phone walks your property and applies the treatments. No corporate dispatcher, no call center, no out-of-state ownership.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. We are a licensed Pennsylvania pesticide applicator (8 categories including general, agricultural, and aquatic) and a Pennsylvania-certified Nuisance Wildlife Control Operator (NWCO). We carry full liability insurance for residential, commercial, and agricultural work. Licensed continuously since 2005.
Do I need a contract?
No. Every estimate is free with no obligation. Most services are billed per visit — cancel or skip any time. We don't run on the long-term contract model that national chains use.
Why does the "beekeeper" thing matter?
Because we're the rare licensed pesticide applicator who also depends on healthy pollinators for our other business. We choose products, timing, and application methods specifically to protect bees. If you have your own bees, a pollinator garden, or fruit trees, we're the right choice.
How quickly can you respond?
Routine work books a few days to a week out. For emergencies — active stinging insect nests, wildlife in living spaces, swarms during bee season — we prioritize same-day or next-day response when at all possible.
