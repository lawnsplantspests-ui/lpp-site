What We Do

Most pest and lawn care companies specialize in one thing. We're licensed for all of it:

  • Lawn Care — fertilization, weed control, crabgrass pre-emergent, core aeration, overseeding, soil testing. Traditional and fully organic programs.
  • Pest Control — residential and commercial pest control for ants, spiders, yellow jackets, hornets, wasps, fly remediation, and seasonal prevention programs.
  • Mosquito and Tick Control — yard barrier treatments, In2Care mosquito stations, and seasonal protection.
  • Plant Health Care — insect control, disease management, soil-applied systemic treatments, dormant oil, and spotted lanternfly management.
  • Nuisance Wildlife Control — Pennsylvania-licensed NWCO services for raccoons, groundhogs, squirrels, skunks, opossums, bats, and snakes.
  • Honey Bee Removal — humane live removal in coordination with The Alleman Apiary, never extermination.
  • Pollinator Restoration — pollinator-friendly lawn programs, pesticide audits, native plant guidance, and bee-safe mosquito control. The only Central PA pest control company that also operates a working apiary.
  • Agricultural Pest Service — fly control for cattle, poultry mite and louse treatments, sheep and goat parasite control, and varroa mite management.
  • Pond and Aquatic Service — licensed aquatic herbicide applications, algae control, and mosquito larvae treatments.

Our Approach

Diagnosis Before Treatment

We walk the property, identify what's actually going on, and recommend treatments based on what your property actually needs — not a one-size-fits-all package.

We're connected to a working apiary, so we choose products and timing that minimize impact on bees and other pollinators. Soil drenches over foliar sprays. Treatments timed around bloom on flowering plants.

Real Organic Options

Our fully organic lawn care program is one of the only true organic-only lawn programs available in Central PA. We also offer reduced-risk and organic-compatible product options across other services.
