Treatments & Safety

Are your treatments safe for kids and pets?
Yes, when applied correctly. Our pest control is perimeter-only — we don't spray inside your home, which already removes the biggest indoor-exposure concern. Outdoor applications use EPA-registered products applied by a Pennsylvania-licensed applicator, and we'll always tell you the re-entry interval. For sensitive situations we offer reduced-risk and organic-compatible product options.
Do you spray inside the home?
No. Our pest control is perimeter-only — we treat the exterior of your home (foundation, eaves, soffits, garage doors, entry points) to keep pests from getting in. No interior spraying, no kitchen treatments, no fumes in your living space. Better for kids, pets, pollinators, and air quality.
Do you offer organic options?
Yes. Our fully organic lawn care program is one of the only real organic-only lawn programs available in Central PA. We also offer reduced-risk and organic-compatible options across plant health care, mosquito and tick control, and agricultural work.
Will your treatments harm bees and pollinators?
We work hard to make sure they don't. We're a working beekeeper through our sister business The Alleman Apiary. We use soil drenches instead of foliar sprays where possible, time applications before or after bloom, and choose products that target the specific pest rather than broad-spectrum products.

Specific Services

Do you handle honey bees?
Yes — but we don't exterminate them. We coordinate with our partner apiary, The Alleman Apiary, to remove and relocate them alive. See our Honey Bee Removal page.
Do you handle mice, rats, or roaches?
No. Rodent control (mice and rats) and cockroach extermination aren't part of our service mix. For either, reach out to a specialist focused on those pests. Squirrels, raccoons, groundhogs, and other nuisance wildlife we do handle as a separate service — see nuisance wildlife.
Do you do tree removal or pruning?
No. We focus on plant health care treatments — insect control, disease management, fertilization, soil work, and spotted lanternfly. For pruning or tree removal you'll need a certified arborist.
Do you service farms and rural properties?
Yes. We provide Agricultural Pest Service including fly control for cattle, poultry mite and louse treatments, sheep and goat parasite control, and varroa mite management for honey bees.
Do you do wildlife removal?
Yes. We're a Pennsylvania-licensed NWCO and handle raccoons, groundhogs, squirrels, skunks, opossums, bats, and snakes.
How quickly can you respond?
Routine work books a few days to a week out. For emergencies — active stinging insect nests, wildlife in living spaces, swarms during bee season — we prioritize same-day or next-day response when at all possible.
