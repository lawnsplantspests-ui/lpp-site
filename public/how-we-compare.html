When LPP Is the Right Call

  • You want one person handling multiple property issues — same diagnosis, same trust, no rotating face.
  • You care about pollinators — you keep bees, have a butterfly garden, or just don't want broadcast sprays anywhere near flowering plants.
  • You want a real organic option for lawn or plant care — not greenwashed marketing.
  • You'd rather not have chemicals applied inside your home, and you're open to the perimeter-only approach to pest control.
  • You don't want to sign a contract just to get pricing.
  • You have multiple property issues and want one phone number instead of four.
  • You appreciate someone who will tell you when a service isn't worth doing on your property.

When LPP Isn't the Right Call (Honest List)

We're not for every job. If any of these apply, hire someone else — and we'll often tell you who:

  • You need tree climbing, pruning, or removal. Hire a certified arborist (ISA-certified, TCIA-accredited) — there are several reputable arborist companies in the Harrisburg and Lancaster areas. We do tree/shrub treatments, not crown work.
  • You have a roach or rodent infestation. Rodent control and cockroach extermination aren't part of our service mix. Call a specialist.
  • You want indoor pest spraying. Our model is perimeter-only. If you specifically want interior application, we're not the right fit.
  • You're outside Central PA. We serve Dauphin, Cumberland, Lebanon, Perry, and York counties. Outside that, we may refer you to a partner.
  • You need same-day pest work on a weekend. Saturdays are emergency-only and Sundays are very limited. For active stinging nests with kids/pets at risk, we still try — but routine weekend work isn't our model.
