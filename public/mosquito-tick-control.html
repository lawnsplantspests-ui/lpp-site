Why Mosquito & Tick Control Matters in Central PA

Central Pennsylvania has the perfect conditions for both mosquitoes and ticks — humid summers, plenty of standing water, and large deer populations carrying ticks into yards. The PA Department of Health reports Lyme disease cases in Dauphin, Cumberland, and Lebanon Counties every year, and West Nile virus shows up in local mosquito samples each summer. A single yard treatment knocks down adult populations. A seasonal program keeps them down all summer.

Yard Barrier Treatments

We treat the resting areas where mosquitoes hide — undersides of leaves, dense shrubs, ornamental beds, shaded areas. Treatments typically last 3–4 weeks before reapplication is needed.

In2Care Mosquito Stations

Targeted bait stations that kill mosquito larvae at every life stage. Uses the mosquitoes themselves to spread larvicide to nearby breeding sites. Far gentler on bees and pollinators than broad-spectrum spraying. We're trained In2Care applicators.

EcoGuard Plus Organic Applications

Fully organic, EPA-exempt essential oil barrier treatment with 30-day residual repellency. Zero synthetic insecticides. Safe for pollinators when applied as directed. As working beekeepers, we wouldn't apply something that kills our own bees.

Tick Control

Targeted treatments for lawn edges, leaf litter, woodlines, and perimeter zones where ticks "quest." For properties bordering woods or fields, we add perimeter treatments where deer and rodents enter the yard.

Breeding Site Reduction

We inspect your property for standing water — clogged gutters, tarps, kiddie pools, bird baths, plant saucers, low spots — and treat or recommend changes for each one.

Special Event Treatments

Outdoor wedding, graduation, family reunion? We do single-application treatments timed 24–48 hours before your event for maximum effect. Call early — weekend slots fill up fast in summer.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does mosquito and tick control cost?
Pricing depends on yard size, vegetation needing treatment, and whether you choose a single treatment or seasonal program. We give free estimates over the phone or on-site.
How often are treatments needed?
Every 3–4 weeks during mosquito season is the standard for full season-long control. After heavy rain you may notice a brief uptick in activity — the next scheduled treatment will catch up.
Are treatments safe for kids, pets, and bees?
Yes, when applied correctly. We avoid spraying flowering plants directly and time applications for early morning or evening when bees are less active. If you keep your own bees, tell us — we'll work around your hive locations.
What is In2Care and how does it differ from traditional spraying?
In2Care uses small stations placed around your property. Female mosquitoes contact a slow-acting larvicide and carry it to actual breeding sites — passively treating standing water you might not even know about. Far less product, targets mosquitoes specifically, works on hidden breeding sites.
Do you offer organic mosquito control?
Yes — and ours actually works. EcoGuard Plus is a fully organic essential-oil barrier treatment with 30-day residual repellency. It's EPA-exempt under FIFRA Section 25(b), made from plant-derived ingredients only.
