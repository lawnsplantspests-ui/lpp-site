Pests We Handle in Harrisburg

Ants

Odorous house ants, pavement ants, carpenter ants. Bait-first approach so the colony, not just the foragers, takes the hit. Particularly common in the inner suburbs every spring.

Yellow Jackets & Hornets

Ground-nesting yellow jackets, bald-faced hornets, European hornets, paper wasps. Same-day emergency response for active aerial nests near foot traffic. Dedicated yellow jacket service page.

Spiders

Web removal plus targeted perimeter treatment. Most common: cellar spiders, common house spiders, the seasonal wolf spider in the fall. We identify before treating.

Seasonal Prevention

Quarterly perimeter program for households that want pests caught at the door instead of treated after they're inside. Most cost-effective approach for older Harrisburg homes with multiple entry points.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm in a row home — does pest control even work when my neighbors aren't treating?
Partially. We do exterior perimeter treatment around your unit — foundation, doorframes, window penetrations, soffits — which stops most ants from getting in from outside. But if your neighbor's unit has an active interior infestation that's pushing through shared walls, we can't address that side. We're up front about what perimeter work can and can't do. Note: we don't treat roaches or rodents at all — for those you'll need a specialist.
