Pests We Handle in Hershey

Ants

Odorous house ants, carpenter ants, pavement ants, and the occasional acrobat ant. We identify the species first — different ants need different products and different bait strategies. Carpenter ant activity in mature Derry Township homes often indicates a moisture issue we'll flag.

Yellow Jackets & Hornets

Ground-nesting yellow jackets in patios and stone walls, bald-faced hornets in shrubs and soffits, European hornets in tree cavities. Same-day response for active nests with kids or pets nearby. Dedicated yellow jacket service page.

Spiders

Web removal plus targeted treatment around foundation, eaves, and outbuildings. Brown recluse is rare in Central PA but we identify and handle if found.

Carpenter Bees

The chronic spring problem for Hershey cedar fascia, deck railings, and unpainted soffits. Treatment targets the galleries; long-term fix involves repainting or repairing.

Seasonal Prevention

Quarterly perimeter treatments for households that want pests handled before they show up indoors. Especially useful for Hershey estate lots with multiple outbuildings.

What We Don't Treat Rodent control (mice and rats) and cockroach extermination aren't part of our service mix. For either, reach out to a specialist focused on those pests. Squirrels, raccoons, groundhogs, and other nuisance wildlife are handled separately under nuisance wildlife.

My ant problem comes back every spring — why?
Most likely you have a nearby colony that the original treatment didn't fully reach. Hershey's mature landscapes are full of mulch beds, decorative stone, and retaining walls that hide satellite colonies. We approach recurring ant problems with bait first (lets the workers carry the active back to the colony) instead of perimeter spray, which only knocks down foragers.
