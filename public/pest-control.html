Common Pests in the Harrisburg Area

Spring

Carpenter ants and pavement ants are the most common callouts. Stink bugs emerge from winter hiding. Spotted lanternfly nymphs hatch.

Summer

Mosquitoes and ticks at peak pressure. Yellow jackets and paper wasps build nests. Spotted lanternfly adults appear.

Late Summer / Fall

Bald-faced hornets reach peak nest size. Yellow jackets become aggressive. Mice begin pushing indoors.

Winter

Rodents and overwintering pests (stink bugs, ladybugs, cluster flies) take refuge in attics and wall voids.
