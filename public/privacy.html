Privacy Policy

Lawns Plants & Pests LLC is a locally owned pest control and lawn care company in Harrisburg, PA. We collect only what we need to answer your questions, schedule services, and stay in touch about your property. We do not sell or share your personal information.

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you call, text, email, or fill out our quote request form, we receive the contact details you choose to share — typically your name, phone number, email address, property address, and a description of what you need help with. If we perform service, we keep records of those visits for scheduling and regulatory purposes. When you visit our website, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may log standard request information such as your IP address, browser, and the pages you visited, which is used to keep the site secure and reliable.

How we use it

We use your information to respond to inquiries, schedule and complete services, send appointment confirmations, and follow up about seasonal treatments. If you opt in to a regular service program (lawn care, pest prevention, mosquito treatments), we keep your schedule on file so we can remind you when it's time. We do not send unsolicited marketing.

What we do not do

We do not sell your information. We do not rent or trade your contact details. We do not share your information with third parties for advertising. We do not run advertising trackers on this website.

Who we share with

We share only what's needed to do the work: payment processors handle payment information directly, and our website is hosted by Cloudflare. Pennsylvania Department of Agriculture regulations require us to keep pesticide application records — those stay with us unless requested by the department. If you need a sister service (like honey bee relocation through The Alleman Apiary), we'll pass along only the details needed to help you.

How long we keep it

Service records (pest applications, lawn treatments, wildlife removals) are kept for at least three years per PA Department of Agriculture requirements, and often longer for ongoing customer relationships. Casual inquiries that don't lead to service are typically cleared within a year.

Cookies

This website does not set tracking cookies. Our hosting provider may use minimal technical cookies for security and performance.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to delete your contact information, opt out of future follow-ups, or send you a copy of what we have on file. Just call, text, or email and we'll take care of it.
