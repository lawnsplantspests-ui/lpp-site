What Carlisle Properties Deal With

Carlisle ranges from the historic borough at the center to the suburban neighborhoods east toward Boiling Springs and Mechanicsburg to the working farms and rural properties out toward Newville and Mount Holly Springs. The South Mountain ridgeline brings its own pressure. Common service calls:

  • Spotted lanternfly on tree of heaven and maples — heavy pressure along the I-81 corridor
  • Deer browse on arborvitae, yews, and ornamentals — especially properties bordering South Mountain
  • Ticks and lyme disease pressure for properties near woodlots, fence lines, and the AT corridor
  • Yellow jackets and bald-faced hornets in older borough homes and rural barns
  • Agricultural pest work — fly control for cattle and poultry, varroa mite management for area beekeepers
  • Aquatic vegetation management on farm ponds throughout Cumberland County
  • Lawn fungal pressure on irrigated yards through humid summers
