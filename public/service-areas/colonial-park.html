What Colonial Park Properties Deal With

Colonial Park is an established Lower Paxton Township residential community east of Harrisburg, with mid-century housing stock, mature street trees, and well-kept residential lots. The neighborhood's age and density shape the typical pest mix. That mix creates a specific set of pest pressures:

  • Spotted lanternfly on tree of heaven, maples, and walnuts throughout the neighborhood
  • Yellow jackets and bald-faced hornets in mature soffits and porch voids
  • Carpenter bees on cedar fascia and deck rails of mid-century homes
  • Lawn fungal pressure on irrigated yards through humid summers
  • Squirrels, raccoons, and bats in attics of older Colonial Park homes
  • Deer browse on arborvitae and yews from nearby wooded edges
