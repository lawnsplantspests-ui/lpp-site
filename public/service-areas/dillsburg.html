What Dillsburg Properties Deal With

Dillsburg is a growing York County community at the foot of South Mountain, with a mix of historic Main Street housing, newer residential subdivisions, and rural-edge properties bordering wooded land. The mountain-edge setting drives a specific set of pest pressures. That mix creates a specific set of pest pressures:

  • Spotted lanternfly on tree of heaven and maples throughout the area
  • Deer browse on arborvitae and yews — heavy from the wooded South Mountain edges
  • Ticks and Lyme pressure for properties bordering woodlots
  • Yellow jackets and bald-faced hornets in older borough soffit voids
  • Carpenter bees on cedar fascia in newer subdivisions
  • Nuisance wildlife — raccoons, groundhogs, skunks — common on mountain-edge properties
