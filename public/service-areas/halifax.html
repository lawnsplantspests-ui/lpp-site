What Halifax Properties Deal With

Halifax is a small northern Dauphin County borough along the Susquehanna with established homes in the borough center and surrounding rural properties out toward Powell's Valley and Wiconisco. Small-town and rural living shape the pest mix. That mix creates a specific set of pest pressures:

  • Mosquitoes along the Susquehanna and Powell's Creek
  • Spotted lanternfly along the rail and Route 147 corridors
  • Ticks and Lyme pressure for properties bordering woodlots
  • Deer browse and nuisance wildlife on rural-edge properties
  • Yellow jackets and bald-faced hornets in older borough soffit voids
  • Agricultural pest pressure on surrounding farms
