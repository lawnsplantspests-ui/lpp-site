What Hershey Properties Deal With

Hershey mixes Derry Township's wooded estate lots with denser neighborhoods around Cocoa Avenue, Chocolate Avenue, and the medical center. Add in surrounding farmland and the Swatara Creek corridor and the pest pressures are wide-ranging:

  • Deer browse on arborvitae and yews — Hershey has heavy deer pressure, especially properties bordering Hersheypark woods or Derry Township's wooded edges
  • Spotted lanternfly in the maple- and walnut-lined neighborhoods along Park Avenue and Areba Avenue
  • Mosquitoes and ticks on properties bordering the Swatara Creek or wooded estate lots
  • Yellow jackets and bald-faced hornets in soffits and ground voids — common late summer call
  • Lawn fungal pressure on irrigated estate lawns through humid summers
  • Nuisance wildlife — groundhogs, raccoons, skunks — especially on properties bordering preserved land
