What Hummelstown Properties Deal With

Hummelstown sits between Hershey and Harrisburg with a mix of historic borough lots, newer South Hanover Township subdivisions, and rural-edge properties along the Swatara Creek. That mix means a wide range of pressures:

  • Spotted lanternfly on the maples and tree of heaven along Hanover and Main Streets
  • Yellow jackets and bald-faced hornets in older borough homes — porch voids, soffits, retaining walls
  • Mosquitoes on properties backing onto the Swatara Creek corridor
  • Deer browse on arborvitae and yews — especially South Hanover Township properties bordering wooded land
  • Carpenter bees on cedar trim and deck rails in newer construction
  • Lawn fungal pressure on irrigated yards through humid Central PA summers
