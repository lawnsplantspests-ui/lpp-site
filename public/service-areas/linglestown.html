What Linglestown Properties Deal With

Linglestown is a historic Lower Paxton Township community north of Harrisburg, with a mix of established neighborhoods around the Square, newer subdivisions off Mountain Road, and properties along the wooded edges of the Blue Mountain foothills. That mix creates a specific set of pest pressures. That mix creates a specific set of pest pressures:

  • Spotted lanternfly on tree of heaven and maples — heavy pressure along the Route 22 corridor
  • Deer browse on arborvitae and yews — strong from the wooded edges north of town
  • Yellow jackets and bald-faced hornets in late summer in older neighborhoods
  • Ticks and Lyme disease pressure for properties bordering Blue Mountain woodlots
  • Lawn fungal pressure on irrigated yards through humid summers
  • Carpenter bees on cedar fascia and deck rails common in newer construction
