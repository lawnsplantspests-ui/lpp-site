What Mechanicsburg Properties Deal With

Mechanicsburg sits in the South Mountain foothills with a mix of established neighborhoods, newer construction off Trindle Road, and rural-edge properties bordering woodlots. That mix creates a specific set of pest pressures:

  • Spotted lanternfly on tree of heaven and maples — heavy pressure along the I-81 corridor
  • Yellow jackets and bald-faced hornets in late summer, especially in older neighborhoods with mature trees
  • Deer browse damage on arborvitae and yews from the wooded edges of South Mountain
  • Lyme disease ticks at any property bordering woods or brush
  • Lawn fungal pressure on irrigated yards through humid summers
  • Carpenter bees on cedar fascia and deck rails common in newer construction
