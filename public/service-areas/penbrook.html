What Penbrook Properties Deal With

Penbrook is a small borough between Harrisburg and Susquehanna Township with established residential neighborhoods, mature street trees, and compact lots. Older housing stock and dense plantings shape the typical pest mix. That mix creates a specific set of pest pressures:

  • Spotted lanternfly on mature maples and tree of heaven on borough streets
  • Yellow jackets and bald-faced hornets in late summer, especially in older porch and soffit voids
  • Carpenter bees on cedar fascia and deck rails
  • Lawn fungal pressure on shaded, irrigated yards in humid summers
  • Squirrels and raccoons in attics — common in older Penbrook homes
  • Ant and spider pressure in basements
